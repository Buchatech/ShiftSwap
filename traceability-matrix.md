## Validation Review Findings

1. **Traceability coverage:** All current requirements are mapped to PB issues, acceptance criteria, test case issues, validation method, evidence placeholder, owner, and status.
2. **Compliance/governance gaps:** Access control policy for employee-ID login and CSV export, audit retention/immutability controls, and notification reliability/SLA definitions are not fully specified.
3. **Ambiguous or weakly testable areas:** Eligibility/qualification rules for claiming, overtime calculation basis, and post-approval correction flow need clarification.
4. **Missing acceptance criteria quality:** Several requirements had high-level acceptance focus only; PB issue bodies were normalized with explicit criteria.
5. **Validation evidence status:** Evidence references are now required and tracked, but most execution artifacts are still pending because test execution has not been completed.
6. **Deferred scope confirmation:** Slack integration, payroll integration, and automatic overtime blocking are formally marked deferred and linked to governance test cases.
